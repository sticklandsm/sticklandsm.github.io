A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

Strengths:

My ability to focus, brought to you by the most Ritalin you legally can legally be prescribed.

I am extremely motivated to complete this course and prove to myself and others that I can achieve higher education when given something I'm passionate about and the right medication.

Programming experience, I've dabbled in this area a lot over the years, and I've always enjoyed and been good at it. The previously undiagnosed ADHD hampered this dream until now.

Limitations:

Even with the previously mentioned massive amounts of meth(ylphenidate). I still find it hard to concentrate for long periods if I'm not doing something I'm invested in.

Hyper focus can be a blessing and a curse. I find it difficult to take the 5-minute breaks that the Tomato person suggests because once I stop it's hard to get back into it.

The severe ADHD also lends itself to disorganization. I'm innately really bad at keeping a schedule, recording my time correctly, and being on time for things. Combined with depression which makes it hard to get out bed sometimes. I'm pretty on top of all of these problems now due to coping methods but there may be the occasional slip up.

I understand that mindfulness and mindful listening exercises are good and helpful to most nuerotypical people. Like an Athiest in a prayer circle I'm fine sitting down and checking my emails or listening to a podcast or something instead.
